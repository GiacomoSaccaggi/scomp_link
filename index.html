THE ASTROMECH ARM STRIKES BACK


In a data galaxy far, far away, scientists and engineers wage an endless war against chaos. Messy pipelines, hyperparameter nightmares, unexplainable models, and the Dark Side of repetitive coding threaten to consume their most precious resource: time.

But the Rebellion has upgraded its arsenal...


SCOMP-LINK

A legendary toolkit forged in the fires of real-world ML battles. Much like the versatile interface of an astromech droid, SCOMP-LINK plugs directly into your data, taking over the flight controls and automating the entire journey from raw chaos to production-ready predictions. No manual steering required.


THE JEDI POWERS

TOTAL AUTOPILOT
It handles the whole mission: profiling data quality, engineering features, selecting the model, tuning hyperparameters, training, validating, and generating reports. One command. Full pipeline.

A DROID FOR EVERY TERRAIN
Regression, classification, clustering, time series forecasting, anomaly detection, text understanding, image recognition. One tool to master them all.

HYPERSPACE NAVIGATION
The built-in decision engine analyzes your data and automatically chooses the fastest, most accurate model. Or unleash Optuna's Bayesian optimizer to explore the hyperparameter galaxy at warp speed.

X-RAY VISION
SHAP values and LIME explanations reveal exactly why every prediction is made. No more black boxes. Full transparency at the feature level.

DRIFT RADAR
PSI and Kolmogorov-Smirnov tests continuously monitor production data. The moment distributions shift, you know—before your metrics decay silently.

FAIRNESS TRIBUNAL
Demographic parity. Disparate impact. Equalized odds. The 4/5 rule. Built-in bias detection ensures your model treats everyone fairly—or tells you exactly where it doesn't.

STRENGTH IN NUMBERS
Why rely on one pilot? SCOMP-LINK combines multiple models into an Elite Squadron using voting and stacking ensembles for superior performance.

MISSION DEBRIEFINGS
After every run, it generates interactive HTML reports. Data quality profiles. Fairness dashboards. Drift charts. Clear visuals—no guesswork involved.


THE WORKFLOW

PHASE 1: RECONNAISSANCE
Profile your data. Find missing values, constants, duplicates, high correlations. Know your terrain before engaging.

PHASE 2: ARMAMENT
Forge new features. Log transforms, polynomial interactions, target encoding, date extraction. Prepare your weapons.

PHASE 3: ENGAGEMENT
Train, optimize, validate. Ensemble if needed. LOOCV, Bootstrap, Walk-Forward for maximum confidence. Save the .scomp artifact.

PHASE 4: INTELLIGENCE
Understand every decision. SHAP and LIME reveal the Force behind each prediction.

PHASE 5: DEPLOYMENT
Load artifact, predict on new data. One call. Preprocessor + model chained automatically.

PHASE 6: VIGILANCE
Monitor drift and fairness continuously. Catch distribution shifts and bias before they cause damage.


ADVANCED CAPABILITIES

12 CLI COMMANDS
Zero-code ML from the terminal: run, predict, explain, engineer, forecast, anomaly, drift, fairness, quality, compare, info, init. Full power without writing Python.

THE .SCOMP FORMAT
One file captures your entire pipeline: model, preprocessor, config, metrics, feature schema, sample data. Save it, ship it, load it anywhere, predict in one call.

TEMPORAL MASTERY
ARIMA, SARIMA, Exponential Smoothing with auto-seasonality detection. Walk-forward cross-validation. Confidence intervals. Time series done right.


BATTLE-HARDENED DESIGN

• Resilient: 185 combat simulations (tests) passing. Zero tolerance for failure.
• Fast: Polars-powered preprocessing at lightspeed.
• Modular: Heavy deps (torch, tensorflow) only loaded when needed.
• Silent: Configurable logging—disappears into the background on demand.
• Typed: Full IDE support with py.typed marker.
• Battle-tested: CI/CD across Python 3.10–3.13. Codecov. Auto-publish to PyPI on tag.
