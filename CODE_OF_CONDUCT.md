# Code of Conduct

## The Jedi Council's Oath

*"Fear leads to anger. Anger leads to hate. Hate leads to suffering."* — Yoda

In this project, we choose the Light Side.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual orientation.

## Our Standards

**Behavior that strengthens the Force:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards fellow contributors

**Behavior from the Dark Side (not tolerated):**

- Trolling, insulting comments, or personal attacks
- Harassment (public or private)
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **giacomo.saccaggi@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated. Maintainers who do not follow the Code of Conduct may be removed from the project.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions, wiki) and in public spaces when representing the project.
